ROBO SOCCER

Can you make a bot as good as Messi or Ronaldo?

PROBLEM STATEMENT 

To build a manual robot capable of scoring as many goals as possible in a given
amount of time. 

General Rules: 
 

1. A team can consist of maximum 4 members.

2. The candidates may or may not be from different colleges.

3. The members must bring IDENTITY CARDS of the college.

4. No person should be a member of two teams.

5. No two teams should participate with the same robot for this event.

6. Any kind of damage to the arena by the robot will lead to direct

disqualification.

7. Any act of misbehaviour or misconduct will lead to immediate

disqualification of the team.

8. The decision of the coordinators will be final and binding.

9. Any or all of the rules are subject to change at any point of time.

Event Rules:

1. The main objective of the event is to score as many goals as possible in a

given time frame.

2. A ball will be kept at the centre of the arena and the robot has to

navigate without touching any of the bricks and score the goal.

3. A goal is considered once the ball crosses the goal line.

4. Touching the bricks will lead to penalty of points.

5. A limited time will be given to the participants in which he/she has to

score the maximum number of goals possible.

6. Each goal will reward points.

7. The participant can opt for a restart at any point during the run. The

score will be reset with an appropriate penalty for restart.

8. The wire must be kept slacked at every point of the run.

9. A trial run, before the final run will be given to the participants if

required to configure the bot according to the arena.

10.Please follow the dimensions specified, as because no changes will be

made in the arena on any request of any participant.

11.Participants cannot hold or grasp the ball with any mechanism at any

point during the run, if done so it will lead to restart of the run with

appropriate penalty for restart.

12.The robot cannot be changed once it has entered the competition. The

same structure dimension and weight will be used throughout the event.

13.Finals will be one to one match between two teams the winning team

will qualify to the next round and the loosing team will be knocked out.

14.Shooting mechanisms will give a bonus point to the participants (i.e. if

the participant can shoot the ball from a distance of at least 15 cm and

not push it towards the goal)

15.Bot dimension (25 x 25 x 25) cm with 10% tolerance, it includes all

peripherals attached to the bot.

16.Maximum voltage between any two points is 24 V (which may be

checked if required).

17.Maximum weight of the bot is STRICTLY 2 KG.

18.AC 220 V will be provided, any other required power sources, extension

cords and power adaptors should be carried by the participant.

19.No use of any Lego or readymade bots; use of so will lead to direct

disqualification.

20.Bots of the participant may be checked anytime; before, during or after

the run for above mentioned criteria.

21.No use of any unfair means, if found will lead to immediate

disqualification.

22.All participants are requested to maintain the spirit of a healthy

competition.
